Bevel Gear

Project Overview

Developed a precision bevel gear system to study angular power transmission and torque transfer between intersecting shafts. The design emphasizes contact mechanics, alignment constraints, and manufacturability, with geometry optimized for smooth meshing and mechanical efficiency.

Innovation & Contribution

I increased the bevel gear's head length to imrpove the durability of bevel gears, which was a huge problem as they are consistently under stress which means they have to be changed frequently.

Engineering Thought Process

This project helped me understand that sometimes the biggest problems often have the most smallest and simplest solutions. The problem where bevel gears fracture or malfunction due to low durability is very dangerous. By simple simulation and stress mechanics, I found that by increasing the bevel head's size by x, the overall stress applied per unit area decreases by 1/sqrt(x)
