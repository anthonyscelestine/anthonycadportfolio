Orbital Lunar Fly-By Mission

Project Overview

This project focuses on the conceptual design and CAD development of a toroidal propeller aimed at reducing tip vortices, acoustic noise, and improving wake efficiency for UAV propulsion systems.

Innovation & Contribution

My innovation lies in adapting toroidal geometry for manufacturable UAV-scale propulsion, exploring blade continuity, curvature constraints, and structural feasibility using CAD-driven iteration.

Engineering Thought Process

The design process began by questioning conventional blade-tip losses and exploring non-planar flow structures. Design decisions were guided by aerodynamic intuition, DFMA constraints, and future simulation potential.
